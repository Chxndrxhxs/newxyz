1. what is Agile?

description-:

Agile is a flexible, iterative approach to building software where you work in short cycles (called sprints), ship frequently, and adapt based on real feedback — instead of planning everything upfront and hoping for the best.

Agile flow representation:-
--------------------------------

1-Project Planning – Define project goals, scope, and overall vision.
2-Product Backlog Creation – List and prioritize all features and tasks.
3-Sprint Planning – Select and organize tasks for the upcoming sprint.
4-Sprint Execution – Develop and complete the planned tasks.
5-Continuous Testing & Integration – Regularly test and integrate work to ensure quality.
6-Sprint Review – Present completed work and gather stakeholder feedback.
7-Sprint Retrospective – Reflect on the sprint to identify improvements.
8-Iteration / Repeat – Continue the cycle with updated priorities and learnings.
