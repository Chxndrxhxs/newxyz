5. what is jira?

description-:

Jira is a project management and issue-tracking tool widely used in Agile teams.
It helps teams plan, track, and manage tasks like features, bugs, and improvements.
Work is organized into tickets that move through workflows (e.g., To Do → In Progress → Done).
It supports Agile practices like Scrum and Kanban with boards and sprint tracking.
Teams use it to improve visibility, collaboration, and overall project control.

Jira Workflow
-------------
1-Create Issue – Add a task, bug, or feature as a ticket in Jira.
2-Backlog Grooming – Prioritize and refine issues before starting work.
3-Sprint Planning – Move selected issues into the current sprint.
4-Start Work (In Progress) – Developer picks a ticket and begins implementation.
5-Code Review / Testing – Work is reviewed and tested for quality.
6-Done – Issue is marked complete after meeting all criteria.
7-Track & Report – Use boards and reports to monitor progress and performance.
