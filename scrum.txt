6. what is scrum?

description-:

Scrum is an Agile framework used to manage and deliver projects in small, iterative cycles called sprints.
It focuses on delivering working software frequently rather than waiting for a big release.
Teams are self-organizing and collaborate closely to achieve sprint goals.
It defines specific roles like Product Owner, Scrum Master, and Development Team.
The goal is faster delivery, adaptability, and continuous improvement.

Scrum Workflow
---------------
1-Product Backlog Creation – List all features, tasks, and requirements.
2-Sprint Planning – Select items from the backlog to work on in the sprint.
3-Sprint Execution – Team builds features within a fixed time (usually 1–2 weeks).
4-Daily Scrum (Standup) – Quick daily sync on progress and blockers.
5-Sprint Review – Demo completed work to stakeholders for feedback.
6-Sprint Retrospective – Reflect on process and improve for next sprint.
7-Repeat Cycle – Start the next sprint with updated priorities.
